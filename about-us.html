About Us

Our History

New American Partnership (NAP) is a non-governmental, non-religious, non-partisan, non-profit 501 (C) 3 humanitarian organization under processing. Our values are attached in the spirit of assistance and the value of community empowering, preparing and giving back to community.

The New American Partnership was established in 2019 by immigrants/refugee’s youth and concerned community member around metro area, all from diverse cultural backgrounds, they came together as one group that cares the communities and their neighborhoods that needs support and help from the community members. The main purpose initialed the new American Partnership is all about to giving back to the community to improve the lives of their families and their communities. Together we can make a difference.

Our Programs

Our Mission



New American Partnership (NAP) is a non-profit organization, helping and empowering society through youth development programs, providing employments and working to reduce the devastating effects of drug abuse and crime rate. Without any profit concerns, we provide trustful services of employments trainings. New American Partnership is working as a responsive community to provide services as youth development programs and crime prevention outreach Social services.


Our Vision:



New American Partnership reaches out to the community to empowerment for justice and equality for everyone. Promote for peace and development to community. Programs.

Youth Programs



The New American Partnership engages East African youth age 14-24 in shaping a positive future through discovering their unique talents and abilities by experiencing meaningful activities, education, Mentoring, building career awareness, and accessing valuable work experiences. Youth can build a career pathway to secure higher wages and expanded occupational opportunities in their futures.
